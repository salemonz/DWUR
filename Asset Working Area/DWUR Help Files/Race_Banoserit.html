Weapon Preference: Phaser

A large flightless bipedal avian race, Banoserit have short legs and exceptionally large hands on long arms. Covered in feathers the Banoserit are rarely seen without most of the body including covered in clothing.

The Banoserit are mostly a dependable species who hold themselves and others to high morale values, reputation is exceptionally important to them.

To be a Banoserit is to accept total responsibility for your own sins and the sins of your ancestors. It is considered that if your ancestors did something morally bad that this behavior is in your blood and you will accept the merciless judgement of your own species.

The Banoserit are gifted with the most exceptional eyes of all species that now travel the stars. They are able to see more than most other eyes are capable and a large part of their brain processes this sensory input. The Banoserit spend great effort to attempt to unlock this ability further for use in scanning the 'skies' of space as an early warning system of danger.

This has led to the development of the Far Sight Center, considerably more effective than most Long Range Scanners.

The Banoserit are found in large, and unusually vertical cities in the deserts of the driest planets.
