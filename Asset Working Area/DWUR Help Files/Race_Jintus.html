Weapon Preference: Rail Gun

Small brown- and gray-skinned rodents, the Jintus often get themselves in some form of conflict, however they are not very enthusiastic about actually fighting. They will often draw in their allies to fight their own battles, using their chosen friends as the intimidation factor in any war.

The Jintus favor races they consider to be strong and able to win wars for them.

Living and hunting in packs, pack feuds and wars are common. Hunting is a sport they are obsessed with. Many Jintus will lose their lives hunting the Terrellion, a dangerous predator from their homeworld 10 times the size of the average Jintus.

Jintus inhabit caves and other natural or intelligently made shelters of ice worlds. Not very good engineers they will kill or drive out occupants of these potential habitats to find warm homes.
