The Shakturi are an extinct race of insectoid aliens that are said to have inhabited the galaxy long ago.

Many ancient myths and legends recount the Shakturi as fearsome invaders from another galaxy far away. They were cunning and merciless aggressors who successfully conquered most of the galaxy. But when they were on the verge of total victory, they suddenly vanished without trace.

At least, that’s what the ancient stories say. Others claim that the Shakturi never really existed, but are instead an invention used to slander all insectoid races as cruel and evil.

The Shakturi preferred weapon is the Shaktur Firestorm and they focus on Power and Shields with Energy Research.
