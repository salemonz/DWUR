Weapon Preference: Phaser

The Yor resemble beetles or cockroaches of Earth.

The Yor rarely communicate with others and seem to value spreading themselves out to other planets above all else.

Yor are not very intelligent but do work well in groups. They do not have a concept of copyright or patent. Stealing ideas is acceptable, advancement in technology comes about because of a common drive towards their own races power not personal gain.

The Yor have years of knowledge of phaser beam technology and have adapted their phaser technology to work on star fighter scale. The Yor Stinger Beam gains more range than comparable Phasers, allowing them to strike first before the enemy has realized they are upon them.

The Yor can be found in the driest deserts, their habitats built upon the sand. These hovels are usually swarming with Yor who do not seem to mind overpopulation levels others would find unbearable.
