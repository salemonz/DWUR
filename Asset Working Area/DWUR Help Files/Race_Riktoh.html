Weapon Preference: Missiles
Research Preference: Speed & Agility

The Riktoh are an average-sized, canine species.

For the Riktoh their greatest importance is wealth, they are always looking for a way to get rich fast and go to great risks to do so. Despite this desire, they generally don't consider taking riches from another being an option, being a mostly law-abiding race. Conversations with a Riktoh generally just concentrate on what belongings they have, how big their homes are and other displays of wealth.

For many Riktoh, the dawn of the space age signals immense opportunity to become extremely rich. Early years as a space-faring race saw large numbers of Riktoh launching themselves into the unknown in ill-equipped space craft, all for the opportunity to be one of the first to find riches beyond their world.

The Riktoh are a bit wary of other races naturally but are usually very willing to trade. They are very dependable friends.

Cautious by nature the Riktoh have pioneered the ability to disrupt Hyperdrive use for a limited range in space.  The Space Denial 5000S was developed as an early-warning system against planetary attacks by outside aggressors.

The Riktoh live in small communities, where land is divided up by wealth. Border disputes are common and within their communities Riktoh will often have fierce rivalries and few friendships.
