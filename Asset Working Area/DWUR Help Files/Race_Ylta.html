Weapon Preference: Beams and Fighters

A somewhat small avian race with gray or dark plumage and a striking physical presence.

They are an aggressive species but often uses quick action to scare others and try and gain advantage.

Long ago, the Yl'ta describe a time when they were a race consumed by greed. They labored, betrayed other species at will and fought wars over material wealth, thinking the acquisition of goods was the sole path to spiritual ascension.

Eventually they were completely defeated by their accumulated enemies. The Yl'ta describe this as their Time of Shadow, as their planet fell under centuries of anarchy and scattered recorded history.

The Yl'ta describe a time of eventual Spiritual awakening, where they were gifted tools of creation and untold power that quickly propelled them back to the stars.

The Yl'ta now seek to know the Galaxy, its wonders and what rarities it hides. Despite the empire's goals and recent change in ethos, its people's happiness and productivity are still largely affected by the wealth the the Yl'ta has access to.
