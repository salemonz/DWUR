Weapon Preference: Fighters/Bombers and Torpedoes

The Caleph are flying Reptiles, large creatures that can have up to a 15 meters wing span. The Caleph vary in size, with more subtle variations in color and looks. The different variations in the species rarely interbreed. Partially due to this, the Caleph have developed a strong caste-based society, with groups defined by physical characteristics that, to the outside observer, might seem too subtle to warrant differentiation.

Because of their natural ability for flight the Caleph are used to dealing and navigating in three-dimensional space. They make extremely good pilots and in space combat they are unequalled in ability.

Their natural flight ability has allowed the Caleph to develop a different type of fighter bay, the All-Dimension Fighter Bay, this allows them to hold and repair fighters much more efficiently. The Caleph have also developed their own fighter craft that are paragons of maneuverability.

Caleph seek out hot places for their homes. They do not have houses or habitats like most races might--they claim tracts of land as "homes." Additionally, this can be as a family or pack--less often as an individual.
