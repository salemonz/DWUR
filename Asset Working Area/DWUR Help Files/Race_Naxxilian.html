Weapon Preference: Phaser

Towering, heavily muscled reptiles with imposing horns and thick bony plates protecting their skulls. Although they are reptilian, Naxxilian bodies are covered in shaggy fur. Their powerful arms and sharp talons make them natural melee fighters.

Though highly aggressive, they can be quite loyal allies if befriended. Overall, Naxxilians tend to be very cautious, though they can be quite friendly once they become familiar with others.

Many Naxxilians live in small tribal encampments. However they also have highly-developed urban areas, filled with advanced industries such as starship construction.

Naxxilians normally live on the tundra wastelands of Ice Glacial planets, but can sometimes also be found in the polar and mountain areas of Continental planets.
