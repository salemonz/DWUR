Weapon Preference: Fighter/Bombers and Torpedoes

A very hardy, relatively short amphibian species, the Tairoshan developed on a home world that harbors frequent large climate shifts because of its two large moons.

Tairoshans are not very intelligent but are extremely optimistic and naïve creatures. Most life on Roshar evolved to support other life because of such adverse conditions, this has made Tairoshans extremely unequipped to mix in the Galaxy because they judge everyone by their own standards, finding it extremely hard to imagine other races treating them badly despite past experience.

Because of their nature they are a very likeable race and other races all love to trade with Tairoshans, often taking advantage of them.

Extremely positive about their space program, Tairoshans believe meeting other life can only be a good thing for them. All Tairoshans are enthusiastic to spread out in the Galaxy.

On Roshan all life has a 12 year metamorphosis cycle because of one of the moons, this still happens throughout the Galaxy because each Tairoshan's cycle synchronizes with other Tairoshans around them. Life goes in to a slowed down docile state while each individual changes sex and finds a new partner to reproduce.

The Tairoshans use mechanical, tentacle like robots for many jobs and have adapted this technology to quickly dock, refuel or load ships. The Rad Coupler allows for extremely efficient docking operations that can be achieved at high speeds.

Tairoshans usually stick together in large communities, able to easily colonize any water based planets.
