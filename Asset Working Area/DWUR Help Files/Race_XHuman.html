Weapon Preference: Gravitic

Biologically and physiologically, XHumans are indistinguishable from Humans. This leads to confusion from other species, though Humans and XHumans insist on the division.

XHuman origins centered around an event known in Human history as the Relakis incident. Relakis was a colony of the Humans that underwent strict quarantine due to an accidental release of an intensely painful and fatal virulent laboratory-grown disease. The disease left millions dead and dying in absolute agony and was nearly impossible to treat once contracted. The Relakis quarantine lasted many generations and was was largely successful at stopping significant spread of the disease outside of the system.

Relakis was abandoned to its fate. Over time, the few Human persevering survivors began developing genetic immunity to the disease, though they still carried genetic scars from exposure to it.

So intense was the fear of a re-emergence of the Relakis disease that nearby Human colonies refused to consider re-opening Relakis to any Human contact. The few residents of Relakis who did travel through Human-controlled space were forced to register and wear encounter suits. They were shunned, turned away and branded "Ex Humans" as a slur.

Defiant, the 'Ex Humans' took up this moniker and used it to describe themselves as a new race entirely: the history of XHumans began.

XHumans culture differs from Human society in many ways. XHumans embrace cybernetic implantation, are much more collectivist and have no scruples about polyamory or group families. XHumans have fewer inhibitions than Humans, seeing them as weak and indecisive.

XHumans and Humans find each other detestable. Something about the extreme familiarity, the very close in looks but also noticeably different continues to make each race intolerant of each other.
