Weapon Preference: Missiles
Research Preference: Power and Shields

The Visareen (used plural and singular) are a generally peaceful race, however they take extreme pride in the outward show of strength. They will make great efforts to have a very large military, and their entire culture is based around military shows, competitions and displays. Most Visareens will grow up with the desire to enter and serve in their military forces in some way.

The Visareen's leader, is considered by many Visareen to be a god, and to the rest they believe he is directly related or gifted by a spiritual being.

The Visareen make a somewhat cold neighbour to other Empires, appearing threatening with their overt displays of strength but in reality they will rarely make any actions to invoke actual conflict.

The Visareen live in large spread-out communities all centered around their own temples built to hear the frequent preaching of their leader.
