Weapon Preference: Gravitic
Research Preference: Speed & Agility, Energy Research

An Enton is a short humanoid with pale, greyish brown skin. They appear to have large heads for their bodies and move in a slow purposeful manner. Other races find Entons hard to empathize with in conversation because of their mostly expressionless behavior.

The Entons are closely related to the Kiadians. The historical genetic relationship isn't clear and both the Entons and the Kiadians have their own ideas why their genes are so similar.

They are a curious race--in technology, other life and the history of the Galaxy. Large parts of their industry are devoted to philosophy, science and discovery.

They are also very cautious, preferring to observe in secret, partly because of a lack of trust in other species but also to not interfere with what they are observing. The Entons find other races to be very quick to fight, this observation has made the Entons grow cautious and be slightly more aggressive than they naturally are for their own defense. (Although this is still much less aggressive than most.)

Enton structures are often very vertical and curved in stature. Most structures and ships are "grown" using engineered non intelligent life. This makes their cities appear very varied and random in design as each building has variation in it's own way.

The Entons have long been using the stealth technology of the Disbelieve Mask.  Created to confuse various sensors the Disbelieve Mask doesn't make the Enton ships invisible but greatly reduces the distance their ships can be detected by sensors by making the ships appear like a natural occurrence or an anomalous signal.

Entons live very solitary lives outside of their work, many jobs for an Enton can involve not seeing another Enton for almost their entire life, their only interaction being through non direct mediums of communication.
