Weapon Research: Fighter/Bombers and Missiles

The Napoar were long ago a race of humanoid aliens. Before being a space-faring race they were able to foresee a massive climate changing event to their home world. The Napoar had not developed the ability to travel between the stars, instead they were able to progress their technology so far that they were able to transfer their consciousness in to cybernetic bodies.

The Napoar developed bodies were extremely hardy and able to withstand extreme heat and weather to survive the massive change to their home world.

In transcending to this synthetic state the Napoar lost many of the evolution developed emotional instinctual behaviors. They were no longer driven by a will to seek power and have lost their aggression.

The Napoar find non aggressive races to be far more agreeable and seek for every race in the Galaxy to find their own place and to coexist and help each other survive. They see no reason why war or conflict is needed at all.

In game terms this race can be one of the fastest to spread to other planets. Because of their new bodies they find it easy to develop the technology to populate different types of worlds. They are extremely passive though and are not the best at war. They will attempt to live in coexistence with other peaceful races.
