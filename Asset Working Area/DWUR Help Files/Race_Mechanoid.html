The Mechanoids are a very strange race – they exist as pure logic, living inside computer networks. They have no physical form, but they can project their presence on holographic displays or video screens.

They are assisted by small robots called Helpernoids, who build and maintain all of the machinery for the Mechanoid civilization.

No one seems to know where the Mechanoids came from – they certainly appear to be the product of an extremely advanced, but now vanished civilization. What happened to that parent civilization? Why did they make the Mechanoids? Not even the Zenox or the Quameno can answer those questions.

The Mechanoids are very reclusive. They describe themselves as guardians of the planet Utopia. This system is a forbidden zone that they fanatically defend. Their only communications are terse warnings to stay clear of their territory.

They have no interest in expansion or growth – they confine themselves to their planet Utopia in their home system. The Mechanoids appear to be very unfriendly and unwelcoming – perhaps it would be wise to stay clear of them.

The Mechanoids preferred weapons are Beams and they have a Balanced approach with Energy Research.
