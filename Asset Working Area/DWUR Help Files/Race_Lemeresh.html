Weapon Preference: Phaser
Research Preference: Power and Shields

The Lemeresh are almost ethereal in appearance. They are large water based life forms.

The Lemeresh live in the deepest oceans of a planet, under the water they build leviathan like ships with extremely powerful weaponry.

Little is known about the Lemeresh outside of their appearance and observing their under water ships. They are insular and secretive, a race of very few words that does not share any information about themselves with others.

Most races fear that if the Lemeresh are able to somehow convert the full array of their aquatic-based technology to the vastness of space, they will be unequalled in power.
