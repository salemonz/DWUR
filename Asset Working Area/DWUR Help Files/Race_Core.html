Weapon Preference: Railguns, Bombardment, Area-of-Effect and Ion

What began as a conflict over the transfer of consciousness from flesh to machines escalated into a war which has a thousand worlds. The Core and the Arm have exhausted the resources of whole galactic sectors in their struggle for total domination. Both sides now crippled beyond repair, the remnants of their armies continue to battle in new regions of space, their hatred fueled by over six hundred years of total war.

This is a fight to the death. For each side, the only acceptable outcome is the complete elimination of the other.

The Core have one consciousness, but several sub-routines to handle mundane affairs. Therefore communication is through a proxy program. No known image of the consciousness exists.

Though once human, The Core now despise all individualized "non-patterned" entities. Their hated rivals, the Arm, are to be eliminated on sight.
